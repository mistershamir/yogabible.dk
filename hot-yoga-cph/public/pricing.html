Which Pass Is Right for You?

First time trying hot yoga?

Start with the KickStarter (599 DKK, 10 classes/3 weeks) if you live in Copenhagen. Visitors should grab the Tourist Pass (895 DKK, 7 days + equipment).

Practice 1-2 times per week?

A 5 or 10-class card gives you flexibility without a subscription. Or the 10 Classes/Month membership (999 DKK) if you want ongoing access.

Practice 3+ times per week?

The Unlimited Monthly Membership (1,249 DKK) or even better, the 6-month pass (6,899 DKK = 1,149/mo) saves you significantly over time.

Going all in?

The 12+1 Month Unlimited Pass is our best value at 969 DKK/month — that's 13 months of unlimited yoga. You save 2,688 DKK compared to monthly membership.

Under 30?

You get 8-19% off everything automatically. No student ID needed — age-based only.

Want to share with family/friends?

Class cards of 60+ classes are shareable (60 classes: 1 person, 100 classes: 2 people, 200 classes: 3 people).
