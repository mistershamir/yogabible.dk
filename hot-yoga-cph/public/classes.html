Hot Yoga Classes in Copenhagen

8 class types, 30-35 weekly sessions in infrared-heated studios. All levels welcome, all classes taught in English.

At Hot Yoga Copenhagen, every class takes place in our infrared-heated studios at 30-35°C. Unlike traditional hot yoga studios that heat the air, our infrared panels warm your body directly — like standing in warm sunlight. This creates a more comfortable breathing environment while still delivering all the benefits of heat: increased flexibility, deeper stretches, and enhanced detoxification.

Hot Vinyasa Flow

60 min All Levels 35°C

Our most popular class. Dynamic, flowing yoga that synchronizes breath with movement through creative sequences. Each class is unique — teachers bring their own style while maintaining a foundation of alignment and mindfulness. You'll build strength, flexibility, and cardiovascular endurance while the infrared heat helps your muscles warm up quickly and stretch deeper. Expect a full-body workout that leaves you energized and centered.

Best for: Anyone looking for a complete yoga experience
Intensity: Moderate to high

Hot Power Yoga

60 min Intermediate+ 35°C

The most challenging class on our schedule. Power yoga builds serious strength through sustained holds, arm balances, inversions, and advanced transitions. The infrared heat adds an extra dimension of challenge and helps you access poses that might feel impossible in a cold room. This class will push your limits while teaching you to find calm within intensity. Come ready to sweat and leave feeling accomplished.

Best for: Experienced yogis seeking a vigorous practice
Intensity: High

Hot Yin Yoga

60 min All Levels 30°C

A slow, meditative practice where you hold poses for 3-5 minutes, allowing gravity and gentle heat to stretch your deep connective tissue — fascia, ligaments, and joints. The lower heat (30°C) creates a cocoon-like atmosphere perfect for turning inward. Yin yoga is the ideal complement to more dynamic practices and is deeply therapeutic for stress, tension, and tight hips/hamstrings. Many of our members call this their favorite class for mental health.

Best for: Stress relief, flexibility, recovery days
Intensity: Low (physically), deep (mentally)

Hot Yoga Basics

60 min Beginner 32°C

Your perfect first class. Basics breaks down foundational yoga poses, breathing techniques, and proper alignment in a supportive, non-intimidating environment. The teacher moves at a pace that allows you to learn and ask questions. You'll gain confidence in the fundamental poses used across all our class types, making this the ideal stepping stone into our full schedule. No experience whatsoever is needed — just show up.

Best for: Complete beginners, returning after a break
Intensity: Low to moderate

Hot Flow & Restore

60 min All Levels 33°C

The best of both worlds. The first half is a flowing vinyasa practice that builds heat and energy, while the second half transitions into restorative poses with longer holds and deeper relaxation. This balanced approach means you get the cardiovascular benefits of flow yoga and the deep-tissue release of yin — all in one session. A favorite for members who want variety without choosing between energizing and calming practices.

Best for: Those who want both movement and stillness
Intensity: Moderate

Hot Sculpt

60 min All Levels 35°C

A high-energy fusion of yoga and bodyweight strength training in full infrared heat. Think squats, lunges, push-ups, and planks woven into yoga flow sequences — all set to a motivating music playlist. Sculpt is designed to tone and shape while maintaining the mindfulness and breath awareness that makes yoga unique. You'll burn serious calories while building lean muscle. No equipment needed — just your body weight and the heat.

Best for: Fitness enthusiasts who want yoga + strength
Intensity: High

Meditation & Breathwork

45 min All Levels 28°C

Pure stillness and breath. This class guides you through pranayama (breathing techniques) and meditation practices in a gently warmed space. Learn techniques like box breathing, alternate nostril breathing, and body scan meditation that you can take into your daily life. The warm room helps your body relax quickly, allowing you to go deeper into meditation without the distraction of cold. Perfect for managing stress, improving focus, and cultivating inner peace.

Best for: Stress management, mental clarity, mindfulness
Intensity: Gentle

Vibro Yoga

45 min All Levels Room temp

A truly unique Copenhagen yoga experience. Vibro Yoga is practiced on vibrating platforms that gently oscillate as you hold poses. The vibrations activate your stabilizer muscles (you'll feel muscles you didn't know you had), deepen stretches through involuntary micro-contractions, and improve balance and proprioception. It's fun, it's different, and it produces results that surprise even experienced yogis. You won't find this class at any other studio in Copenhagen.

Best for: Anyone seeking a unique, effective workout
Intensity: Moderate
